Frequently

What is Argentum NFT?

Argentum Alpha NFT is a collection of algorithmic generated contemporary art designed by Argentum group and minted as NFT’s on the network. They are constructed from various geometric shapes, lines and colors in different % which determines their rarity. The purpose of Argentum Alpha NFT is to provide economic benefits to their owner’s. Aiming to be the world safest NFT collection. We achieve this by providing to our community buy back options at the same price as the minting purchase price measured in USD.

What do I get?

After the mint you will receive your Argentum alpha NFT to your wallet. The rewards will be paid out to your wallet on a monthly basis, at the first business day of each month.

How do I get one?

Go to our website argentumnft.com connect your wallet, choose your desired tier and category and click mint, approve the transition ease-in-out in your wallet, and… congratulations! You are now a proud owner of the Argentum Alfa NFT.
